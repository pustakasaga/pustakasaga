Pharmaceutical chemistry is a combined scientific discipline of chemistry and pharmacy involved in the design, isolation, synthesis, analysis, identification, development of natural and synthetic materials used as pharmaceutical drugs, which can be used for therapy. This field also carries out studies on existing drugs, in the form of chemical-physical properties, structures, and structure and activity relationships (HSA). This book provides a foundation for understanding Analytical Chemistry, Synthetic Chemistry, Physical Chemistry, and also Pharmaceutical Analysis and its technical applications in the laboratory.
