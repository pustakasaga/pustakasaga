Digitalization in marketing means using the internet as a source to market your business. Common digital marketing platforms include email marketing, blogging, social media marketing, paid Google ads, search engine optimization (SEO), vlogging, affiliate marketing, etc.

Businesses nowadays even ask customers to share their opinions and ideas to improve products or services. Digitization helps the B2B industry by allowing businesses to talk or pitch directly to other businesses, CEOs, and those in leadership positions.
